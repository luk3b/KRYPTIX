How edm is made

EDM is music that is made with tools like "samplers, synthesizers, effects units and MIDI controllers".
These help EDM artists create cool beats and rhythms that eventually get turned into music,
but in the 80's it was mostly "manipulating MIDI data". Its much easier though to create music
with better technology that allows EDM artists to create music at a much faster rate.

EDM is made alot with MIDI controllers, and you may be confused what these are.
Well an MIDI controller is "any hardware or software that generates and transmits
Musical Instrument Digital Interface". MIDI controllers have probably been seen in
in your life, or have been heard of like "Keyboards are by far the most common type of
MIDI controller". So, MIDI controllers are not uncommonly heard and come in different varietis.
